Penned by Henry Louis Stevens in 1886, The Comic Natural History of the Human Races was the first chromolithographed book in America and the first to represent birds as people.

  • The Woodpecker
    (Picus gihonis major.)
  • The Lark
    (Alauda noctivagans.)
  • The Hum-Bug
    (Phlebotomus stultorum.)
  • The Florence Humming Bird
    (Trochilus politicus.)
  • The Masonic Terrapin
    (Obstetrix auctorum.)
  • The Vampyre
    (Psalmicantor vehemens.)

Published by Eric Portis.
